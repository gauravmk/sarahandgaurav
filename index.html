Wedding Day Schedule
11:00 AM

Ceremony @ City Hall

Address: 301 E Huron St
|
Dress Code: Garden Semi-Formal
Join us as we get married by the mayor of Ann Arbor! We'll meet inside the City Hall Lobby at 11:00 AM. The ceremony will start promptly at 11:30 AM and will end by 12:00 PM.

There are nearby parking structures (Fourth and Washington is likely the closest) and metered street parking for anyone driving in.
1:00 PM

Reception @ Burns Park

Address: 1300 Baldwin Ave
|
Dress Code: Garden Semi-Formal
Our reception style is inspired by the warmth and casualness of family meals. Please join us at 1:00 PM as we set out plates and silverware in advance of food arriving at 1:30 PM.

In the spirit of utilizing our local community resources, we'll have Ann Arbor Public Library-provided lawn games and other activities to keep the party going until we cut the cake at 3:30 PM.

Our food will be from nearby Indian and Middle Eastern restaurants. We've done our utmost to accommodate various dietary preferences, and there will be both vegan and non-vegan options.

In lieu of speeches, we'll provide custom letterhead and writing utensils for you to share your thoughts, well-wishes, and advice with us. We plan to bind these into a memento book for us to treasure.

There is parking next to the Burns Park structure and along the neighboring streets.
7:00 PM

Afterparty @ Downtown Ann Arbor

Address: 139 Ashley Mews Dr
|
Dress Code: Casual
A (low-key) night on the town: Between Summer Streets and Top of the Park, Ann Arbor in June is an exciting time. We'd love to get dinner and drinks with siblings and friends, and enjoy a warm summer evening in Ann Arbor together. We'll meet at our house first, and then head to Main St / Top of the Park.

Street parking is available on neighboring streets (note: after 6:00 PM, street parking is free!). The Fourth and William public parking structure is also very close ($1.50/hr).
